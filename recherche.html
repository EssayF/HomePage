Research

Research interests:

  • IoT: networks and optimization, Fog/Edge Computing, Cloud Computing, Placement problem
  • QoS and energy consumption in cloud systems (Stochastic model, optimal control policy, Load Balancing and scheduling algorithm)
  • Performance evaluation and network dimensioning: Queuing systems, Markov chain, Simulation
  • Model and aggregate large data flow, Stochastic comparison
